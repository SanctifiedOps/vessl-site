Frequently asked questions

Clear answers, no fluff. If you have more questions, reach out anytime.
Get started Get started
  • Is VESSL for beginners?
    Yes. The system starts simple, then layers progression as you build confidence.
  • What equipment do I need?
    A pair of dumbbells or kettlebells is enough to start. Bodyweight options are included.
  • Can I train around an old injury?
    VESSL is mobility-first with scalable options. Always follow your clinician's advice.
  • How long are sessions?
    Most sessions run 30-45 minutes with clear pacing so you can stay consistent.
  • Can I cancel anytime?
    Yes. No long contracts. Founding pricing is yours for as long as you stay.

    Launching soon. Drop your email and we will send early access first.
